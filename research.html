Center for Policy, Research, and Development Studies

The Talisay City College is a vibrant community of higher education research leaders in various disciplines, committed to improve the quality of life of the underserved and under privileged sectors of the society. The research community of TCC shall be independent, autonomous and visionary thinkers for the furtherance of their respective higher education disciplines in the international and global setting.
